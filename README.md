# Hospitality Dashboard Analysis

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze hotel booking data to understand revenue performance, occupancy trends, cancellation patterns, customer behavior, and overall hotel business performance. This dashboard helps management make data-driven decisions to improve operational efficiency, increase revenue, reduce cancellations, and optimize room utilization.

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Data Cleaning
* Dashboard Design
* Data Visualization
* KPI Analysis
* Business Intelligence Concepts

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Bookings: 134,590
* Total Revenue: ₹1,70,87,71,229
* Occupancy Rate: 58%
* Cancellation Rate: 25%
* RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room)
* ADR (Average Daily Rate)
* Capacity Utilization %

---

## Dashboard Features

### 1. City-wise Revenue Analysis

Analyzed revenue generated across major cities including Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi, and Hyderabad to identify top-performing locations and revenue-driving markets.

### 2. Hotel-wise Revenue Analysis

Compared revenue generated by different hotel properties to identify high-performing hotels and underperforming properties.

### 3. Booking Status Analysis

Tracked booking outcomes such as:

* Cancelled
* Checked Out
* No Show

This helps identify cancellation trends and customer booking behavior.

### 4. Class-wise Revenue Distribution

Analyzed contribution from room categories such as:

* Elite
* Premium
* Presidential
* Standard

This helps understand which room segment drives maximum revenue.

### 5. Capacity Utilization Analysis

Compared:

* Total Capacity
* Successful Bookings
* Utilization Percentage

This helps optimize operational efficiency and improve room occupancy.

### 6. Trend Analysis

Monitored month-wise performance for:

* Occupancy %
* ADR
* RevPAR

This helps identify seasonal patterns and business performance trends.

---

## Key Insights

* Mumbai generated the highest revenue among all cities.
* Checked-out bookings contribute the highest percentage of successful bookings.
* Weekend bookings generate stronger revenue compared to weekdays.
* Elite room class contributes the highest share of revenue.
* Cancellation rate of 25% indicates a major area for operational improvement.
* Some hotel properties show significantly lower performance and require business attention.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Reduce Cancellation Rate

Introduce early booking discounts, better cancellation policies, and customer engagement strategies to reduce cancellations.

### Improve Weekday Occupancy

Launch weekday promotional campaigns and corporate booking offers to improve occupancy during low-demand periods.

### Focus on High-Revenue Room Categories

Increase marketing efforts for premium and elite room categories to maximize profitability.

### Improve Underperforming Properties

Identify operational gaps in low-performing hotels and optimize pricing, customer experience, and service quality.

### City-Level Expansion Strategy

Focus business expansion and premium service offerings in high-performing cities like Mumbai for maximum ROI.
